Mission

Amplifying Human Potential Through AI

Monday Labs exists to transcend human limitations and expand capabilities across communities and age groups through intelligent AI solutions. Technology should amplify abilities, not replace them. We build AI-powered tools that remove barriers, enhance individual capabilities, and democratize access to advanced solutions.

Approach

Human‑Centric, Accessible, Practical

  • ▸Human‑centric — technologies that enhance rather than replace human capabilities
  • ▸Universal accessibility — solutions designed for all ages and communities
  • ▸Practical innovation — AI usage that solves real‑world problems with immediate impact
  • ▸Barrier removal — breaking down limitations that restrict human potential
  • ▸Simplification through AI — distilling complex processes into intuitive experiences
  • ▸Adaptive intelligence — systems that learn and personalize to individual needs
  • ▸Cross‑generational design — interfaces that work seamlessly across age groups
  • ▸Community‑bridge technology — connecting users rather than isolating them
Products

Talka — AI‑Powered English Speaking Companion

Talka is our flagship project — an AI‑driven English communication assistant designed to help adults speak confidently, naturally, and fast. Built for busy professionals and lifelong learners — especially users aged 40 and above — Talka focuses on immediate real‑world speaking ability rather than grammar drills or gamified distractions.

  • ▸Conversational learning — natural dialogues and adaptive guidance that let users start speaking right away
  • ▸Powered by Gen AI — uses large language models, speech tech for smooth, human‑like interaction
  • ▸Innovation ecosystem — developed under the NVIDIA Inception Program, with planned cloud integrations for scale
  • ▸Human‑centric by design — simplifies communication instead of complicating it, empowering learners of any age
